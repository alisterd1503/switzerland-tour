Our Itinerary

Your complete day-by-day adventure

Day 1

Oxford → Strasbourg

Travel through France and Belgium into the Alsace region

  • Coach departs Oxford early morning
  • Ferry crossing from Dover to Calais
  • Scenic drive via Belgium towards Strasbourg
  • Hotel check-in followed by dinner

Day 2

Strasbourg → Rhine Falls → Lucerne

Enter Switzerland and experience Europe’s most powerful waterfall

  • Depart Strasbourg after breakfast
  • Stop at the Rhine Falls with a boat tour to the viewing rock
  • Continue to Lucerne and explore the lakeside promenade
  • Dinner and overnight stay in the Lucerne region

Day 3

Mount Pilatus & Lucerne

A full day of Alpine scenery and lakeside charm

  • Ride up Mount Pilatus by cable car or cogwheel railway
  • Enjoy panoramic mountain views and optional walks
  • Return to Lucerne for free time in the Old Town
  • Dinner and overnight stay

Day 4

Lucerne → Zurich → Black Forest

City exploration and a scenic journey back into Germany

  • Morning visit to Zurich’s historic centre
  • Explore the lakeside, churches, and old streets
  • Continue to Germany’s Black Forest region
  • Dinner and hotel stay in the Black Forest

Day 5

Black Forest → Oxford

Return journey through France and across the Channel

  • Breakfast and check-out
  • Drive across the German and French countryside
  • Ferry from Calais to Dover
  • Late-evening arrival back in Oxford
